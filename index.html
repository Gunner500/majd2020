About us:


Majd2020 is a voluntary team located in Syria, founded on 14/2/2020 by Ghalia Al-Tabaa in the memory of her mother, Majd Niazi.
The team aims to plant one million trees across Syria, as well as help the people in need.

Why do we plant?


Every human civilization started with planting and farming which led to its success in other life's fields.
And because we wished to see beautiful planted roads while travelling, visiting friends or going to university, we looked after making this wish come true, and became a team that plants beauty wherever it goes.

Our Vision:


We beleive that planting trees and saving the enviroment isn't just an enviromental responsibility, but also the chance to build a strong community that cares about improving itself and looks after positive change through:
1- fighting climate change through increasing green spaces.
2- spreading enviromental awareness through presentations talking about the importance of forests, water resources and protecting living creatures.
3- encouraging social engagement through building a bridge of cooperation between the society, non-profit organizations and foundations through participating in our agricultural campaigns.
